
Test Case 1: Search and Add to Cart 


Open Amazon website.

Enter "laptop" in the search bar.

Click the magnifying glass icon or press Enter.

Click on the first search result to view details.

Select a color option from the available choices.

Click "Add to Cart" button.

Verify that the item is added to the cart.

Close the cart overlay.
